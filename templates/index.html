Bridging Tradition with Technology

Our research focuses on the development of a low-resource language model for Ibaloi. Utilizing the RASA Open Source framework, we have developed a specialized intent classification and entity extraction pipeline.

  • Custom Entity Extraction for Ibaloi Dialects
